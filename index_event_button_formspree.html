My Culinary Story

Hello! I'm Simon Sithole, but most know me as Chef Si. I'm a passionate chef with a journey steeped in the art of flavor and the joy of sharing food. My culinary adventure began in Pretoria, inspired by the vibrant food culture and the rich, diverse tastes of South Africa. This early inspiration quickly blossomed into a lifelong dedication to creating exceptional dining experiences under my brand, Exquisite Culinary Touch.

Over the years, I've honed my skills, blending classic techniques with innovative approaches to bring a unique touch to every dish. My philosophy is simple: food should tell a story, evoke emotions, and bring people together. I believe in sourcing the freshest, seasonal ingredients and transforming them into memorable masterpieces that delight the senses.

Beyond the kitchen, my greatest joy comes from seeing the smiles on my clients' faces. Whether it's a romantic dinner for two or a vibrant corporate event, I pour my heart into every detail, ensuring a seamless and spectacular culinary journey for you and your guests.

This journey has been a wonderful race, fueled by a deep LOVE for cooking. I consider my work a beautiful art form, and it's my dream to share it with the world. While my skills are already highly recognized, I believe this is only the beginning. I am relentlessly driven to acquire new techniques and master every facet of this industry, ensuring that every dish I create is an exquisite experience.
